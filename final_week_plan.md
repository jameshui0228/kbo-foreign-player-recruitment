## Final Selection Rule

Do not select the flashiest candidate or message. Select the candidate set that:

- best matches slot-specific evidence;
- survives source split checks;
- has feasible availability;
- has clear risk caveats;
- avoids duplicate profiles across the three slots.

## Robustness Checklist
