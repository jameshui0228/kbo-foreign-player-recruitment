# Three Slot Core Messages v1

Generated: 2026-06-12 KST

Goal: define a distinct, data-driven message for each acquisition slot: one foreign hitter, one foreign pitcher, and one Asian quota player.

## Executive Frame

SSG should not use the three slots as three generic "good player" searches.

The project should present them as a portfolio:

| slot | job | core message |
|---|---|---|
| Foreign hitter | Fix offensive transition states | Buy the missing bridge before RISP |
| Foreign pitcher | Stabilize the weekly game script | Buy an ABS-native six-inning starter |
| Asian quota | Create cheap optionality | Buy insurance against rotation failure, not another ace fantasy |

The deeper message:

> SSG's roster problem is not one hole. It is a sequencing problem. The offense is good after it reaches RISP, the pitching staff collapses before the game can settle, and the new Asian quota can either become a smart option layer or another overloaded shortcut. Each slot must solve a different sequence failure.

## 1. Foreign Hitter Message

### Message Name

**The Runway Converter**

### One-Line Claim

> SSG does not need a generic clutch hitter. It needs a hitter who turns first-base traffic into scoring-position pressure.

### Korean Presentation Wording

> SSG의 타격 문제는 득점권 해결사가 없다는 것이 아니다. SSG는 이미 득점권에서 리그 최상위권이다. 진짜 병목은 1루 주자가 있을 때 득점권으로 넘어가는 공격 경로가 외야/DH 쪽에서 끊긴다는 점이다. 그래서 외인 타자는 홈런형 거포보다, 1루 주자를 득점권으로 올려놓는 브릿지형 OF/DH여야 한다.

### Evidence

- SSG RISP OPS: .831, rank 1st.
- SSG runner-on-first OPS: .649, rank 10th.
- SSG runner-on-first OBP: .304, rank 10th.
- RISP minus runner-on-first OPS gap: +.182, largest in KBO 2026.
- High-leverage OF usage with runner on first: .383 OPS, 52 PA, 0 RBI.
- DH bridge with runner on first: .463 OPS.

### Candidate Translation

Look for:

- OF/DH fit, not a pure 1B-only bat unless the bat is exceptional.
- RHP production through OBP plus gap damage.
- Runner-on-first skill: low GDP risk, line-drive/gap contact, usable opposite/center-field damage.
- Two-strike survival in transition states.
- Plate discipline that keeps innings alive without removing the long-ball threat.

Do not over-prioritize:

- RBI totals without base-state context.
- Raw HR totals in hitter-friendly environments.
- A hitter whose value appears only in already-RISP situations.

### Presentation Tagline

> 득점권 해결사가 아니라, 득점권을 만들어주는 타자.

## 2. Foreign Pitcher Message

### Message Name

**The ABS-Native Load-Bearing Starter**

### One-Line Claim

> SSG should stop scouting foreign starters by reputation and velocity; in the ABS era, the target is a six-inning starter whose command survives the rule-book zone.

### Korean Presentation Wording

> SSG의 외인투수 문제는 단순히 평균자책점이 높다는 것이 아니다. 선발이 5이닝을 버티지 못해 경기 전체가 너무 빨리 압축되고, 불펜과 공격이 동시에 압박받는다. 여기에 KBO는 완전 ABS 리그이기 때문에 '제구가 좋다'는 평판도 다시 검증해야 한다. 외인투수는 150km보다, ABS가 봐도 스트라이크가 되는 공을 반복해서 던지고 3-ball 카운트와 피홈런을 줄이는 6이닝 안정화형이어야 한다.

### Evidence

- SSG starters: 4.55 IP/G, ERA 5.68, WHIP 1.60, OPS allowed .783.
- Starter ERA, WHIP, OPS allowed, IP/G, and outs/start all rank 10th.
- Short starts under 5 IP: 26 of 61 games, 42.6%.
- Win rate after short starts: .308.
- Win rate after non-short starts: .514.
- Import-slot starters: ERA 6.17, WHIP 1.73, OPS allowed .821.
- Domestic starters: ERA 5.06, WHIP 1.44, OPS allowed .731.
- KBO ABS public result data shows the 2024 zone became much stricter and more rectangular than 2023: `beta` +176.4%, `r` +640.1%.

### Candidate Translation

Look for:

- Recent starter workload: 80-90 pitch history and 20+ PA/game profile.
- Zone rate and first-pitch non-ball rate above starter baseline.
- Low three-ball pitch rate.
- BB+HBP% preferably below 8%.
- HR% preferably near or below 2.5%.
- Early-inning wOBA allowed and RISP wOBA allowed better than starter median.
- Hard-hit/barrel suppression while throwing enough strikes.

Do not over-prioritize:

- Velocity without zone command.
- MLB service time without recent starter workload.
- "Strike thrower" language without pitch-level validation.
- A reliever being stretched out unless workload evidence supports it.

### Presentation Tagline

> 강한 공보다, ABS가 스트라이크로 인정하는 공을 6이닝 반복하는 투수.

## 3. Asian Quota Message

### Message Name

**The Option Layer**

### One-Line Claim

> The Asian quota should not be asked to solve the ace problem. It should be used as a low-cost option layer that protects SSG from the next rotation shock.

### Korean Presentation Wording

> 아시아쿼터는 1선발을 싸게 사는 제도가 아니다. 20만 달러 상한의 4번째 외국인 슬롯이라면, SSG가 사야 하는 것은 스타성이 아니라 옵션 가치다. 즉 선발이 또 무너지거나 부상이 생겼을 때 3-5이닝을 즉시 흡수하고, 불펜 과부하를 막고, 필요하면 선발-불펜을 오가는 ABS 커맨드형 브릿지 투수여야 한다.

### Evidence

- The Asian quota is structurally different from a regular foreign-player slot: it is a lower-cost fourth foreign-player lane.
- SSG's 2026 problem shows why this matters: foreign/import starter instability forced the staff to treat substitute/depth pieces like rotation pillars.
- SSG bullpen has the heaviest workload: 4.25 IP/G.
- Short starts force 5.36 bullpen IP on average; non-short starts force 3.43 bullpen IP.
- External message discovery ranked "Asian quota optionality trap" as the second-strongest non-STATIZ message.

### Candidate Translation

Preferred archetype:

- Command-first swingman or long-relief/starter bridge.
- 3-5 inning absorption ability.
- Prior NPB/CPBL/Australia/KBO-like strike-zone adaptation.
- Fast visa/availability path and lower transition risk.
- Low BB+HBP%, low HR%, low three-ball exposure.
- Role humility: useful as a flexible option, not marketed as a savior.

Alternative archetype only if pitcher pool is weak:

- Contact/defense OF who specifically supports the foreign hitter's runway-converter job.
- Must add baserunning/defensive value, not duplicate a corner power bat.

Do not over-prioritize:

- Former-name value.
- A starter-only player who cannot relieve.
- A hitter who duplicates the foreign hitter's profile.
- A player whose only appeal is being cheaper than a regular foreign player.

### Presentation Tagline

> 아쿼는 1선발 복권이 아니라, 시즌 중 붕괴를 막는 옵션 보험.

## Slot Separation

| decision question | foreign hitter | foreign pitcher | Asian quota |
|---|---|---|---|
| What are we buying? | offensive transition | weekly stability | optionality and shock absorption |
| Main failure solved | runner-on-first dead zone | short starts and ABS-command risk | overloading cheap/depth slots |
| Best role | OF/DH runway bat | six-inning starter | command swingman / long bridge |
| Key metric family | on-first, GDP, RHP, two-strike | zone, BB/HBP, HR, workload, early/RISP | multi-inning workload, availability, role flexibility |
| Bad version | generic slugger | velocity-only starter | fake ace |
| Good sentence | "득점권을 만드는 타자" | "ABS에 맞는 6이닝 투수" | "붕괴를 막는 옵션 보험" |

## Final Project Message

> SSG should use the three acquisition slots as a sequenced solution: the foreign hitter creates scoring-position pressure, the foreign pitcher keeps the game stable long enough for that pressure to matter, and the Asian quota protects the roster from the next pitching shock without pretending to be an ace.
